The Grand Arabian
Odyssey

Overview

The ultimate expression of Arabian luxury and exploration. Over three weeks, you will traverse the most breathtaking landscapes of Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Oman. From the ancient Nabataean tombs of AlUla to the futuristic skyline of Dubai, and the serene wadis of Oman, this is a once-in-a-lifetime expedition curated for the most discerning travelers.

Highlights

  • Private chartered flights between major destinations.
  • Exclusive after-hours access to the UNESCO World Heritage site of Hegra (AlUla).
  • A multi-day private yacht charter along the Arabian Gulf.
  • Residency in the most exclusive desert resorts and our custom Khaymah Royal Camps.
  • Curated dining experiences with Michelin-starred chefs in remote desert locations.

Who is this for?

Designed for those who demand the absolute pinnacle of luxury, privacy, and comprehensive cultural immersion. This is for the traveler who wants to see the entire Arabian Peninsula without compromising on a single detail of comfort or exclusivity.

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 01 - 03
Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Destination: Riyadh
Hotel: Four Seasons Hotel Riyadh at Kingdom Centre
Transportation: Private Maybach Chauffeur

  • 08:00 - 10:00: Breakfast at the hotel.
  • 10:00 - 13:00: Explore the birthplace of the Kingdom at Diriyah, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Lunch at Najd Village for authentic Saudi cuisine.
  • 15:00 - 18:00: Visit the National Museum and Murabba Palace.
  • 18:00 - 20:00: Sunset views from the Sky Bridge at Kingdom Centre.
  • 20:00 - 22:00: Private dining at the Globe.
Day 04 - 07
AlUla

Destination: AlUla
Hotel: Habitas AlUla
Transportation: Private Charter Flight (2 hrs) & Luxury 4x4

  • 06:00 - 09:00: Sunrise hot air balloon ride over the surreal rock formations.
  • 09:00 - 11:00: Breakfast at the resort.
  • 11:00 - 14:00: Private, after-hours access to Hegra's monumental tombs.
  • 14:00 - 16:00: Lunch at Tama restaurant.
  • 16:00 - 19:00: Explore the Old Town and Elephant Rock at sunset.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Dinner at Maraya Social by Jason Atherton.
Day 08 - 11
Dubai & Abu Dhabi

Destination: Dubai & Abu Dhabi, UAE
Hotel: Burj Al Arab Jumeirah
Transportation: First Class Flight (2 hrs) & Helicopter

  • 08:00 - 11:00: Fly to the UAE. VIP airport transfer.
  • 11:00 - 13:00: Check into the Burj Al Arab.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Lunch at Al Muntaha.
  • 15:00 - 17:00: Helicopter tours over the Palm Jumeirah.
  • 17:00 - 19:00: Private viewing at the Louvre Abu Dhabi.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Exclusive desert safari in the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve.
Day 12 - 16
Muscat & Jabal Akhdar

Destination: Muscat & Al Hajar Mountains, Oman
Hotel: Alila Jabal Akhdar
Transportation: Flight to MCT (1 hr) & Luxury 4x4

  • 08:00 - 11:00: Travel to Oman. Explore the capital, Muscat.
  • 11:00 - 13:00: Visit the Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque and Mutrah Souq.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Lunch at Bait Al Luban.
  • 15:00 - 18:00: Ascend into the Al Hajar Mountains to Alila Jabal Akhdar.
  • 18:00 - 20:00: Hike through ancient terraced villages and pomegranate orchards.
  • 20:00 - 22:00: Dinner at Juniper Restaurant.
Day 17 - 21
Salalah & Departure

Destination: Salalah, Oman
Hotel: Al Baleed Resort Salalah by Anantara
Transportation: Domestic Flight (1.5 hrs) & Private Chauffeur

  • 08:00 - 11:00: Fly south to Salalah, the perfume capital of Arabia.
  • 11:00 - 13:00: Check into the beachfront resort.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Lunch at Mekong.
  • 15:00 - 18:00: Explore lush wadis, pristine white-sand beaches, and the ancient ruins of the frankincense trade route.
  • 18:00 - 20:00: Conclude your epic journey with a private beachside farewell gala.
  • 20:00 - 21:00: (Day 21) Private transfer to the airport for your departure flight.
