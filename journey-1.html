7 Days / 6 Nights

The Empty Quarter
Escape

Overview

Venture into the Rub' al Khali, the largest continuous sand desert in the world. This 7-day expedition is designed for those who seek profound silence and the raw beauty of untouched nature. Experience the shifting sands, sleep under a canopy of unpolluted stars, and discover the ancient survival secrets of the Bedouin.

Highlights

  • Private 4x4 dune bashing and navigation masterclass.
  • Exclusive stargazing sessions with an expert astronomer.
  • Traditional falconry display at dawn.
  • Luxury mobile tented camp that moves with your journey.
  • Authentic Bedouin feast prepared over an open fire.

Who is this for?

Perfect for thrill-seekers, passionate photographers, and anyone looking for an absolute digital detox. If you want to disconnect from the modern world and reconnect with nature in its most majestic form, this is your journey.

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 01
Arrival in Dubai

Destination: Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve
Hotel: Al Maha, a Luxury Collection Desert Resort & Spa
Transportation: Private S-Class Chauffeur (45 mins from DXB)

  • 14:00 - 15:00: VIP Airport Meet & Greet, fast-track immigration.
  • 15:00 - 16:00: Private transfer to the desert resort.
  • 16:00 - 18:00: Resort check-in and acclimatization in your private pool suite.
  • 18:00 - 19:30: Late afternoon wildlife drive to spot Arabian Oryx.
  • 19:30 - 21:00: Welcome dinner featuring traditional Emirati cuisine under the stars.
Day 02
Journey to Liwa

Destination: Liwa Oasis
Hotel: Qasr Al Sarab Desert Resort by Anantara
Transportation: Luxury 4x4 SUV (3.5 hours)

  • 08:00 - 09:30: Gourmet breakfast overlooking the dunes.
  • 09:30 - 13:00: Depart Dubai, heading south towards the legendary Liwa Oasis.
  • 13:00 - 14:30: Stop at ancient forts and enjoy a private oasis picnic lunch.
  • 14:30 - 16:00: Arrive and check into Qasr Al Sarab, an architectural marvel.
  • 16:00 - 18:30: Sunset camel trek along the towering red dunes.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Fine dining experience at the resort's signature restaurant.
Day 03
Deep Desert Immersion

Destination: The Empty Quarter (Rub' al Khali)
Hotel: Exclusive Mobile Khaymah Royal Tent
Transportation: Specialized Desert 4x4 (2 hours)

  • 07:00 - 08:30: Dawn falconry display followed by a traditional Arabic breakfast.
  • 09:00 - 11:00: Off-road expedition deeper into the Empty Quarter.
  • 11:00 - 13:00: Arrive at the private Khaymah Royal Tent camp setup.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Leisure time and a light Bedouin-style lunch.
  • 15:00 - 18:00: Extreme dune bashing and sandboarding with expert guides.
  • 18:30 - 21:00: Campfire dinner and guided stargazing with a resident astronomer.
Day 04
Bedouin Heritage

Destination: The Empty Quarter (Rub' al Khali)
Hotel: Exclusive Mobile Khaymah Royal Tent
Transportation: Camel Trekking & Walking

  • 07:00 - 09:00: Sunrise meditation and breakfast in the dunes.
  • 09:00 - 12:00: Guided desert walk learning ancient Bedouin tracking and survival skills.
  • 12:00 - 14:00: Return to camp for rest and a traditional lunch.
  • 14:00 - 17:00: Cultural workshop: Arabic coffee roasting and henna art.
  • 17:00 - 19:00: Sunset photography session on the highest dunes.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Feast prepared over an open fire, accompanied by traditional Oud music.
Day 05
Return to Civilization

Destination: Abu Dhabi
Hotel: Emirates Palace Mandarin Oriental
Transportation: 4x4 to Highway, then Luxury Sedan (3.5 hours)

  • 07:30 - 09:00: Final desert breakfast and camp departure.
  • 09:00 - 12:30: Emerge from the desert and travel to the capital city, Abu Dhabi.
  • 12:30 - 14:30: Check into the opulent Emirates Palace and enjoy a light lunch.
  • 14:30 - 17:00: Relax at the private beach or indulge in a signature spa treatment.
  • 17:00 - 19:00: Private yacht cruise along the Abu Dhabi Corniche at sunset.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant within the palace.
Day 06
Abu Dhabi Culture

Destination: Abu Dhabi Cultural District
Hotel: Emirates Palace Mandarin Oriental
Transportation: Private Chauffeur

  • 08:00 - 09:30: Breakfast at the hotel.
  • 09:30 - 12:00: VIP guided tour of the breathtaking Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.
  • 12:00 - 14:00: Lunch at a premium waterfront restaurant on Saadiyat Island.
  • 14:00 - 17:00: Private art tour at the Louvre Abu Dhabi.
  • 17:00 - 19:00: Return to hotel to refresh and relax.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Farewell dinner overlooking the Arabian Gulf.
Day 07
Departure

Destination: Airport (AUH or DXB)

  • 07:00 - 09:00: Final leisurely breakfast at the Emirates Palace.
