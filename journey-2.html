Overview

A profound two-week journey tracing the ancient trading routes of the Arabian Peninsula. This itinerary seamlessly blends the rugged beauty of the Omani mountains with the pristine dunes of the UAE. You will explore ancient forts, vibrant souks, and hidden oases, gaining an intimate understanding of the region's rich cultural tapestry.

Highlights

  • Guided exploration of Nizwa Fort and the surrounding ancient souks.
  • Private luxury dhow cruise through the Musandam Fjords.
  • Helicopter transfer over the Hajar Mountains.
  • Exclusive access to a working camel breeding farm.
  • Three nights in our signature Khaymah Royal Tent in the deep desert.

Who is this for?

Ideal for cultural enthusiasts, history buffs, and luxury travelers who want a balanced mix of adventure, education, and world-class relaxation. It offers a deeper dive into the history of the region without sacrificing comfort.

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 01 - 02
Dubai & Northern Emirates

Destination: Dubai & Sharjah
Hotel: Jumeirah Al Naseem
Transportation: Private S-Class Chauffeur

  • 08:00 - 10:00: Breakfast overlooking the Burj Al Arab.
  • 10:00 - 13:00: Private guided tour of the historic Al Fahidi neighborhood and Dubai Museum.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Traditional Emirati lunch at the Sheikh Mohammed Centre for Cultural Understanding.
  • 15:00 - 17:00: Abra ride across Dubai Creek and exploration of the Gold & Spice Souks.
  • 17:00 - 19:00: Sunset private yacht cruise along the coastline.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Fine dining experience at Pierchic.
Day 03 - 05
Musandam Peninsula

Destination: Musandam, Oman
Hotel: Six Senses Zighy Bay
Transportation: Luxury 4x4 (2 hours) & Private Dhow

  • 07:30 - 09:30: Scenic drive crossing the Oman border into the mountainous Musandam Peninsula.
  • 09:30 - 13:00: Board a traditional luxury Dhow for a cruise through the spectacular fjords.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Fresh seafood lunch prepared on board.
  • 15:00 - 17:00: Snorkeling in crystal-clear waters and dolphin spotting.
  • 17:00 - 19:00: Check into Six Senses Zighy Bay (optional paragliding arrival).
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Dinner at Sense on the Edge, perched high on the mountainside.
Day 06 - 08
Muscat & Nizwa

Destination: Muscat & Al Hajar Mountains
Hotel: Alila Jabal Akhdar
Transportation: Domestic Flight (1 hr) & 4x4 (2.5 hours)

  • 07:00 - 09:00: Early flight to Muscat. Visit the Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque.
  • 09:00 - 12:00: Drive inland to the ancient capital of Nizwa.
  • 12:00 - 14:00: Traditional Omani lunch in Nizwa town.
  • 14:00 - 17:00: Explore the historic Nizwa Fort and the bustling Friday cattle market.
  • 17:00 - 19:00: Ascend the Al Hajar mountains to check into Alila Jabal Akhdar.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Dinner featuring locally sourced mountain ingredients.
Day 09 - 11
Wahiba Sands

Destination: Wahiba Sands Desert
Hotel: Desert Nights Camp (Luxury Tent)
Transportation: Desert 4x4 (3 hours)

  • 08:00 - 10:00: Breakfast and descent from the mountains.
  • 10:00 - 13:00: Journey into the copper-colored dunes of the Wahiba Sands.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Check into the luxury desert camp and enjoy a light lunch.
  • 15:00 - 17:00: Meet local Bedouin families and learn about their nomadic lifestyle.
  • 17:00 - 19:00: Sunset camel ride across the rolling dunes.
  • 19:00 - 21:00: Traditional Omani Shuwa (slow-roasted lamb) dinner under the stars.
Day 12 - 14
Abu Dhabi & Departure

Destination: Saadiyat Island, Abu Dhabi
Hotel: The St. Regis Saadiyat Island Resort
Transportation: Flight to AUH (1 hr) & Private Chauffeur

  • 08:00 - 11:00: Morning flight from Muscat back to the UAE (Abu Dhabi).
  • 11:00 - 13:00: Check into the beachfront resort and relax.
  • 13:00 - 15:00: Lunch at Buddha-Bar Beach.
  • 15:00 - 18:00: Private tour of the Louvre Abu Dhabi and cultural district.
  • 18:00 - 20:00: Farewell dinner at a premium restaurant.
  • 20:00 - 21:00: (Day 14) Private transfer to the airport for departure.
